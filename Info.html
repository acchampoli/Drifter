Born on Cape Cod, and raised all over the New England region, Alexandra Champoli’s craft is rendered from the inspiration she amasses through travel, human interaction, and artistic collaboration. For the last seven years, she has established herself in the Boston area in the field of photography and production. Alexandra’s passions lie within the realms of street fashion, the hip-hop scene, culture, restoration, retouching, painting, and writing. Her expertise in Adobe Photoshop, InDesign, Illustrator, Lightroom, Capture One, Atom, Github, and various scanning systems allows her to fulfill the creative needs of her clients. She gains an edge on her competition from her hands on experience managing art galleries and photo labs. Her technical experience comes from darkrooms, computer labs, wedding photography, and portraiture. Through all her diverse work, Alexandra is continuously drawn to complex cooperations between body, psychology, technology and taboo.

In her personal life, Alexandra gravitates towards live music, illustration, web design, mystery, thrillers, puzzles, discussing strategy, social phenomena, and discovering information about different cultures and new technologies. If you are interested in collaborating or contracting with Alexandra, feel free to reach her at acchampoli@gmail.com!
